About BiteRush

We're on a mission to revolutionize food delivery and connect communities through the universal language of great food.

Our Story

From Clone to Creation

BiteRush was created as part of my journey into full stack development. Inspired by popular food delivery platforms like Swiggy and Zomato, this project helped me understand the structure and design behind real-world applications.

At this stage, BiteRush is a frontend prototype built using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap 5. The goal is to replicate the user experience of a modern food delivery app while I continue learning backend technologies like SQL, Java, and Spring Boot to bring it to life.

This project reflects my dedication to building real-world skills and serves as the foundation for more advanced development ahead.
